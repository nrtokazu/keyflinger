About
=====
KeyFlinger is an on screen keyboard for Android phones. It is designed around
single and multi-touch finger gestures. Each key is capable of 5 independent
gestures, a simple press and a swipe in the four cardinal directions. If your
phone is capable of real multi-touch, then KeyFlinger? can do multiple gestures
that are chorded together to compose a key.
